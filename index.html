01 — Overview

Race weekend, redefined.

Montreal for F1. Spent the day trackside at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve. That night Glenfiddich hosted their Beyond the Track dinner for the Aston Martin F1 partnership. Completely different energy from the track. Small room, good whisky, good people.

Bal Interdit at the Ritz the next night. BOSS x Aston Martin after that. Four events in three days — each with a distinct brand activation, each captured across a 20-slide carousel that positioned Montreal race weekend as the ultimate convergence of motorsport, fashion, and luxury hospitality.

"Montreal during race weekend just becomes a different city."

04 — Experience

Everything provided.

Motorsport

Circuit Gilles Villeneuve

A full race day trackside at Montreal's iconic street circuit on Île Notre-Dame. Paddock access, the Aston Martin garage, and trackside views of Alonso and Stroll pushing the AMR25 through the Wall of Champions section. The Montreal skyline as the backdrop to one of the calendar's most demanding circuits.

Spirits & Hospitality

Glenfiddich — Beyond the Track

Glenfiddich's intimate dinner for the Aston Martin F1 partnership. An elevated whisky experience with custom cocktails, a full Aston Martin F1 car on display in the venue, green velvet lounges overlooking the Montreal skyline, and the Glenfiddich hospitality bar. Completely different energy from the track — small room, good whisky, good people.

Nightlife

Bal Interdit at the Ritz-Carlton

Montreal's most exclusive race weekend event. The Ritz-Carlton's grand ballroom transformed with dramatic red and purple lighting, Laurent Perrier champagne bar, a Mercedes-AMG reveal under crystal chandeliers, and performers in illuminated glass boxes. Hundreds of guests in black tie. A drone show finale visible from the terrace. The kind of night that defines F1 weekend culture.

Fashion

BOSS x Aston Martin F1

The intersection of fashion and motorsport. BOSS's activation for the Aston Martin F1 partnership — where the race weekend's daytime energy transitions into evening fashion. A brand partnership that sits naturally at the crossroads of performance, style, and the F1 lifestyle.
